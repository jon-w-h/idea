Manifesto
Why we exist

Something has been lost
in the way we speak
to one another.

Not the desire to reach each other. If anything, it has intensified — a hunger in our culture for something real, something that names what we are actually experiencing, something that closes the distance rather than performs the closing of it.

The Human Project was founded on the conviction that stories built around genuine human flourishing are more powerful than stories built around fear, outrage, or division. And that the movements aligned with that conviction deserve the intelligence, the infrastructure, and the craft to reach the people who are already, quietly, waiting for permission to believe that something better is available.

This is our founding declaration. It describes what we are building, why we believe it works, and who we are as a team.

The Intelligence Layer

We know what will
move people before
we make it.

A proprietary content prediction model trained on thousands of pieces of political and advocacy content with behavioral outcome data attached. Not analytics — prediction. The difference between a rearview mirror and a map.

RAG + Frontier Model

Fine-Tuned Foundation Model

Open-weight model fine-tuned on the annotated corpus directly. Learns the relationship between annotation variables and behavioral outcomes.

80%+ Target accuracy · Months 4–8

What We Do

Three ways we
serve the work.

Every capability we build serves the same mission. Every piece of content produced feeds the model. Every prediction made improves the next piece.

01
Capability 01

Movement Content

Short-form video engineered to move people from awareness to action. Not engagement — action. Every piece instrumented, distributed, and annotated. The flywheel turns: content generates data, data improves the model, the model improves the content.

02
Capability 02

Content Intelligence

Pre-production prediction. Before a frame is shot, we tell you whether a concept will drive the specific behavioral outcome you need. Twenty years of editorial judgment encoded as machine-learnable signal.

03
Capability 03

Movement Infrastructure

Distribution strategy, creator networks, message architecture, and the intelligence tools that give emerging movements the playbook to compete.
